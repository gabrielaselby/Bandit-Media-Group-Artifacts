Operations

9 skills /operations:
/operations:status-report
Status Report
Weekly or monthly leadership update with KPIs, green/yellow/red health, risks, and action items. Turns a pile of tracker activity into a readable narrative.
/operations:process-doc
Process Doc
Formalize a process that lives in someone's head. Flowcharts, RACI, and SOPs for handoffs or audits, including the edge cases.
/operations:runbook
Runbook
Turn tribal knowledge into exact step-by-step instructions for a recurring task, with troubleshooting, rollback, and escalation paths.
/operations:process-optimization
Process Optimization
Analyze a slow or clunky workflow, find the bottleneck, and lay out a leaner version with fewer steps.
/operations:capacity-plan
Capacity Plan
Workload analysis and utilization forecasting. Stress-test whether upcoming projects fit the people you have, and whether to hire or deprioritize.
/operations:risk-assessment
Risk Assessment
Identify, score, and mitigate operational risks for a project, vendor, process, or decision. Builds a risk register.
/operations:vendor-review
Vendor Review
Evaluate a vendor with cost analysis, risk, and a recommendation. TCO breakdown and negotiation points before sign-off.
/operations:change-request
Change Request
Propose a system or process change with impact analysis, rollback plan, and stakeholder comms ready for review.
/operations:compliance-tracking
Compliance Tracking
Track requirements and audit readiness across SOC 2, ISO 27001, GDPR, and the like. Documents what's done and what's open.
